Chicken Butter Masala

Chicken Butter Masala is another tasty food of North India when it is served along with Naan or Rumali Roti. The different flavor of fenugreek leaves or Kasturi methi and the chicken cooked in butter is tasty food for any non-vegetarian.

Rajma-Chawal

Most north Indian famous dish comes with Rajma Chawal with the iron and protein-rich food constituents. It comes with kidney-shaped red beans accompanied with steamed rice or whole Indian meal for the entire family
